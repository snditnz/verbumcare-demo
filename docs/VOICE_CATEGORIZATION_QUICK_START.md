# Voice Categorization Quick Start Guide

## 5-Minute Setup

### Step 1: Record Your First Voice Note (1 minute)
1. Open a patient's information screen
2. Tap the microphone button
3. Say: "Patient's blood pressure is 120 over 80, heart rate 72"
4. Tap stop

### Step 2: Wait for Processing (30 seconds)
- Watch the notification banner show processing progress
- AI will transcribe speech and extract vital signs data

### Step 3: Review and Confirm (2 minutes)
1. Tap the notification or review queue badge
2. Check the transcript is accurate
3. Verify extracted vital signs data
4. Tap "Confirm & Save"

### Step 4: Check Patient Records (30 seconds)
1. Navigate to patient's vital signs
2. Confirm your data appears correctly
3. Note the timestamp and source

### Step 5: Try More Complex Recording (1 minute)
Record: "Patient Tanaka-san took 5mg amlodipine this morning. She reports lower back pain level 4. Assisted with bathing due to mobility issues."

Expected result: AI should extract medication, pain assessment, and ADL data.

## Essential Voice Commands

### Vital Signs
- "Blood pressure [systolic] over [diastolic]"
- "Heart rate [number] beats per minute"
- "Temperature [number] degrees [Celsius/Fahrenheit]"
- "Weight [number] kilograms"

### Medications
- "Administered [drug] [dose] [route] at [time]"
- "Patient took [drug] [dose] [when]"
- "Applied [drug] [dose] [location]"

### Pain Assessment
- "Pain level [0-10] in [location]"
- "Reports [sharp/dull/burning] pain"
- "Pain [improved/worsened] after [intervention]"

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Transcript is wrong
**Solution:** Edit the transcript, then tap "Re-analyze"

**Problem:** Data not extracted
**Solution:** Use more specific medical terms and numbers

**Problem:** Wrong patient association
**Solution:** Always record from patient's screen or mention patient name clearly
